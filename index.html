Cybertruck Storage Zones

The Tesla Cybertruck has more unique storage compartments than almost any vehicle on the road. Here's a quick guide to where you can add organizers:

Frunk (Front Trunk)

The powered frunk offers roughly 7.1 cubic feet of lockable, weatherproof storage. Best used with insulated cooler bags or cargo nets to keep items from sliding.
